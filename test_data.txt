Company / System Name
---------------------
Northwind HomeCare Portal

Company Background
------------------
Northwind HomeCare is a mid-sized healthcare services company with 180 employees across three regional offices. The company manages home-care appointments, caregiver scheduling, patient intake forms, invoices, and partner referrals through an old internal web portal.

The portal was built years ago by a small contractor team. It still works for daily operations, but releases are slow, outages are difficult to diagnose, and leadership wants to modernize the platform while exploring safe AI-assisted workflows.

System Description
------------------
Legacy web portal used by operations staff, caregivers, billing coordinators, and external referral partners. The system handles patient profile lookup, appointment scheduling, document uploads, invoice status, caregiver assignment, and basic reporting.

The portal is business-critical during working hours. Staff currently use spreadsheets and email to work around missing features.
