About ArchSec

A modernization advisor for teams carrying yesterday's systems into tomorrow.

ArchSec helps users document existing websites and applications, identify outdated technology and process gaps, and generate practical upgrade recommendations with cost, effort, requirements, and diagrams.

Built for clarity

The platform turns vague modernization concerns into scoped issues that stakeholders can understand and approve.

Designed for legacy reality

It accepts imperfect systems, manual workflows, missing documentation, old dependencies, and budget constraints.

Focused on action

Recommendations include migration steps, costs, skills, risks, and diagrams so teams can move from analysis to execution.

Who should use it?

Small businesses with aging websites or internal portals.

Consultants preparing modernization proposals for clients.

Engineering teams replacing manual testing and deployment workflows.

Security teams evaluating technical debt and AI-readiness risks.
